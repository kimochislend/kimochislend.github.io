Kimochi Slend

A downloadable game


ABOUT THIS GAME

Story
A long, long time ago, there was a kingdom that was not recorded inside any literature.
It is said that the king married a princess from the East, and after the arrival of this Eastern princess, strange things began to happen, creatures grew leaves and be controlled... Then what happened no one knows, only that the kingdom just disappeared.
Outside the castle of this kingdom, a little girl was born from a Kimochi, and a woman who claimed to be her mother summoned the girl to her side ......


Game Features

※Classic Metroidvania game.
-The world can be explored freely.
-Players' decisions will lead to different story endings.

※Cool and fun combat
-Excellent combat feel and challenging monsters.
-Increasingly improve yourself as the adventure progresses, gaining new skills and reaching unknown aera.
-Cleverly match your skills and equipment to challenge your enemies in your own unique way.

※Beautiful world with a complex story
-Flower-themed 2D graphics, quality animation and music together create a vivid fantasy world.
-“Kimochi Slend" bravely advances in the unknown world, and as the adventure progresses, "Kimochi Slend" will face the origin of everything.
