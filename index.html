ADVENTURE

Kimochi is a little fox who loves to eat cherries very much. One day, when he fell asleep, his supplies were stolen! Now he has to go through many levels and collect all the cherries to get his supplies back.

Casual

Embark on an exciting journey through dense jungles, air clouds and lava caves! To complete all the levels and get your berries back, Kimochi will have to show his dexterity, speed and ability to solve puzzles.

Indie

Run, jump, slide on walls, cling to ropes and dodge various traps and enemies! Collect magic stars on each level that will unlock new characters.
